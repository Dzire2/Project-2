How Can We Help You?


Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I submit my work?

    To submit your video or audio work, navigate to the submission section of our homepage and follow the instructions provided.

  • What file formats are accepted?

    We accept a variety of file formats including MP4, MP3, AVI, and WAV. If you have a different format, please contact us for further assistance.

  • Is there a submission fee?

    No, submitting your work to BASATA is completely free of charge.

  • How long does the review process take?

    The review process typically takes 1-2 weeks. You will be notified via email once your work has been reviewed.

  • How can I contact BASATA for more help?

    If you have any other questions or need further assistance, please contact us at support@basata.com.
